Japanese Restaurant in Orchard Road

酒菜けりあ (Keria Izakaya) is a traditional Japanese restaurant located in Orchard Road, Singapore at Cuppage Plaza. We specialize in authentic izakaya-style dining, offering fresh seafood delivered from Japan and a curated selection of premium sake.

Conveniently located near Somerset MRT, our Orchard Japanese restaurant is perfect for business dinners, small gatherings, private celebrations, and casual Japanese dining experiences.

Meet the Proprietress

Seino Hatsuho

Originally from Sapporo, Hokkaido, she trained in Japanese cuisine in Japan before moving to Singapore, where she has lived for over 20 years.

In September 2012, she opened Sakana Keria in Orchard with the mission of bringing authentic Japanese izakaya culture to Singapore.

“My grandparents ran an izakaya in Hokkaido. We hope to continue serving heartfelt Japanese cuisine here in Orchard.”
